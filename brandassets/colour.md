## Dynamic / Accent Colour

Any colour in limited, targeted application.

Dynamic colour is used only in limited strategic applications across Campaign, CRM, and social. This ensures our design stands out yet doesn't dilute our overarching brand look and feel.

**Accessibility:** Keep accessibility in mind when sampling accent colours. Ensure black or white text maintains minimum contrast ratios for small and large text when placed over any chosen accent colour.
